CAPS 2011 grid classifications

June 10, 2011

 

In this version of Massachusetts CAPS (v. 3.0), land use and wetlands are combined into

one layer (luwet); other layers (e.g. roads, streams, dams) are separate. The same

coding scheme is used for a the complete landcover (land), with the addition of

developed types (roads, railroads, culvert, and dam). An alternative version (lands)

includes streams on top of roads. For post-processing, a richer classification is used

(postland); see below.
